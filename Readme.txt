Description: Files for the manuscript "Managing Failure: How Should We Govern Problems Arising
from AI/ML Medical Devices?" (Babic, Cohen, Li, Ouellet, and Stern, 2025).

Created: January 29, 2025
Authors: Melissa Ouellet and Ariel D. Stern

To use data from this research paper, please download: 
a) The final dataset of 823 unique 510(k)-cleared devices between 2010 and 2023, classified as Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning approved by the FDA.
b) The corresponding 943 adverse events reported (MDRs for short) between 2010 to 2023 for these 823 devices
